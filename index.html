01 / About

Eight Years. Five Continents.
One ERP.

I'm a Senior D365 Finance & Operations Consultant based in Cheltenham, UK, with a dual background as a Chartered Accountant (ICAI) and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Solution Architect Expert (MB-700). I've delivered end-to-end ERP implementations across pharmaceuticals, renewable energy, manufacturing, IT services, healthcare, and retail sectors.

My work spans the full project lifecycle — from requirement workshops and FRD authoring through configuration, UAT, data migration, and post go-live support. I've led rollouts across UK, Europe, Middle East, North America, and Asia Pacific.

Beyond consulting, I build tools for the Microsoft ecosystem using AI-assisted development to ship products that solve real problems I encounter on the job.

I thrive where precision matters — where a misconfigured financial dimension, a broken intercompany flow, or a flawed posting profile can have real consequences. That's where I deliver clarity, stability, and systems that work the first time.
